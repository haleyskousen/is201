Studing the Restoration of the Gospel of Jesus Christ About The Trip

Beginnig on April 30 2024 you and 23 other classmates will begin a three week course designed to prepare you for your time away. These classes will take place on Tuesday and Thursday from 1:00pm-2:50pm. On May 22 you will meet your travel group in Missouri no later than 1:30 pm. Travel and sleeping arrangements and the majority of meals are already orgnized. Student is expected to be a full particpate in on-site learning and to contribute to the success of the trip. On June 6th the trip will end with travel back to Salt Lake or other location and an online final exam will be opened to complete the courses.

Student is responsible for:

  • $500 Program fee
  • Tuition for two spring classes
  • Booking and paying for travel arrangements to and from Missouri
  • Expenditures for roughly 23 meals while on the trip
  • Any other expenditues accumulated while traveling such as souvineers
