The History of the Restoration Trip Locations

After spending two nights in Independence Missouri we will travel to Liberty Missouri. From there we make our way to Nauvoo Illinois stopping at Adam-ondi-Ahman and the location of the Haun's Mill Massacre on our way. With the exception of same day time travel the majority of the rest of the trip will take place in Nauvoo Illinois where we will study the saints time in that area. We will learn about the important historical events that furthered the efforts of the Restoration of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

During this trip you will fall in love with the heritage of faith that comes with the restoration of the Gospel. You will deepen your testimony in Jesus Christ and the reality of Joseph Smith as his servant in these latter days. You will understand the events that led thousands of inspired and faithful saints to join together in the restored gospel of Jesus Christ.
